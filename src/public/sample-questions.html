Sample Questions for AI Extractor Testing

This page shows the format of questions that work well with the AI extractor.

1. What is the SI unit of electric current?
(A) Volt
(B) Ampere
(C) Ohm
(D) Watt
Correct Answer: (B) Ampere
Explanation: The SI unit of electric current is Ampere (A).
2. Which of the following is a vector quantity?
(A) Speed
(B) Distance
(C) Velocity
(D) Time
Correct Answer: (C) Velocity
Explanation: Velocity is a vector quantity as it has both magnitude and direction.
3. What is the chemical formula of water?
(A) H2O2
(B) HO
(C) H2O
(D) H3O
Correct Answer: (C) H2O
Explanation: Water consists of two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom.
